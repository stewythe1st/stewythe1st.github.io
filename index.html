About Me

I am a software engineer focusing on C/C++ programming, with experience in embedded systems and system design. I have developed control code and user interfaces for systems of all kinds, often with a focus towards autonomy and unmanned operation.

My passion is writing software that connects to the physical world: systems, controls, and interfaces to things that fly, drive, dig, or otherwise do the work to keep our modern world moving forward.
